FOODS TO AVOID

The keto diet is all about increasing calories from fat and going very low carb.
That means following a restrictive, keto-friendly food list.

Here are some of the foods you should avoid on keto:

  • Sugary foods: Soda, fruit juice, smoothies, cake, ice cream, candy, etc.

  • Grains or starches: Wheat-based products, rice, quinoa, pasta, pizza, bread, cereal, etc.

  • Fruit: High-carb fruit, including bananas, pineapples, tangerines, and grapes, except small portions of berries like strawberries

  • Beans or legumes: Peas, kidney beans, lentils, chickpeas, etc.

  • Root vegetables and tubers: Potatoes, sweet potatoes, carrots, parsnips, etc.

  • Low-fat or diet products: These are highly processed and often high in carbs

  • Some condiments or sauces: These often contain sugar and unhealthy fat

  • Unhealthy fats: Limit your intake of processed vegetable oils, mayonnaise, etc.

  • Alcohol: Due to their carb content, many alcoholic beverages can throw you out of ketosis

  • Sugar-free diet foods: often high in sugar alcohols, which can affect ketone levels in some cases and tend to be highly processed

  • Milk

  • Refined oils, Margarine, Artificial trans fats
